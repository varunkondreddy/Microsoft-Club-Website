About the Club

Microsoft Club, SRM is a technical club, under the wing of Microsoft, showcasing and nurturing talents under the well-known and esteemed name of the organisation, to carry forth and express it’s legacy. We conduct events all over campus (and outside campus as well), inspiring students to pursue knowledge, in their own unique ways. Along with this, we aspire to give the push they need through our club’s work, and through training in the various domains that we provide.

Technical (ML, App and Web) Creatives
Sponsorship Marketing and Public Relations

The members of our core team have over two years of experience in their respective domains working for different clubs, events and teams. We have also trained 100+ students by providing them a platform to learn, grow and excel in their interested domains.

Exploring and emphasising on a new era of technology is what makes us stand out from others. We aim to upgrade learning, so as to make easier methods of gaining knowledge available to students all over campus. We carry a trusted and widely known brand name that also helps us stand apart from the crowd and ensures that people to put their trust in us and we have never let that name down in 4 years of our existence in SRM.
